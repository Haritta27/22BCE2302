Recycle Reboot for ZERO WASTE

Current recycling methods fail to address the efficient disposal of items taking an example as in our college where we aren't able to dispose used notebooks and unwanted clothes, resulting in increased waste accumulation. Limited awareness and access to reuse and repurpose options make environmental issues worse. We need an easier way to deal with this. Recycle reboot make this possible.This website have three platform Recycle platform, Reuse Marketplace and donate hub.

Recycle platform

Digital platform connecting recycling firms and households. Users schedule pickups for recyclables at their convenience. Prices for recyclables are displayed, and money is credited automatically after recycling. The users will redirect to the reuse marketplace with ideas of reusing the products they can continue and use the ideas as reference and continue to go to Reuse marketplace or skip and proceed with Recycle platform.

Reuse Marketplace

In this platform ideas will be given for the reuse the waste materials. The users can make a new product out of it and use it for themself or they can sell in this platform and earn some money.

Donate hub

This platform provides an online marketplace for users to donate unwanted items. We thoroughly check their quality before listing them at discounted prices, promoting reuse and extending product lifespan.

This website is made with : HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and ReactJS. It offer a seamless user experience. With dynamic functionality and elegant design, it promotes sustainability through recycling, reusing, and donating. It encourages eco-friendly actions like recycling, reusing, and donating. Plus, it helps users learn about sustainable living in a friendly way, making a positive impact on the environment.
